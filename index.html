About JSK Creation

Supplying the makers behind the jewellery.

JSK Creation sources and sorts raw jewellery-making materials for artisans, home businesses and small manufacturers. Every batch of beads, stones and findings is checked before it's packed, so what arrives at your bench matches what you ordered.

We keep both bulk and small quantities in stock, because a sample order and a production order deserve the same care.
